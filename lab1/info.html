My Improvements

format & styling

The format and styling was inspired by the Revolve template. I wanted to keep the format and styling similar to the template, but I also wanted to make it my own. I changed the colors, fonts, and spacing to make it more appealing to the eye. I added features such as carousel, videos, and a striped table. Also I created new sections of content using the bootstrap grid system.

content & images

The content and images were chosen to be relevant to the topic of surfing. I wanted to include information about the sport, the history, and events that are happening around the world. I also wanted to include images that were appealing and interesting to look at.

why surfing

I chose surfing as the topic of my website because I am a big fan of the sport. I grew up surfing in the US and Ireland in spots such as Rye Rocks, The Point, Naragansett, Jenness, The Wall, and Lahinch. I hope to travel to more surf spots in the future and continue to improve my surfing.
